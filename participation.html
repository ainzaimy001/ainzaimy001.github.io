PARTICIPATION




I visited the National Archives with friends for an assignment on organizing information during my second semester. It was our first time there, but the experience was valuable for learning about research and organization. We researched historical documents, aided by experts, and analyzed records, recording pertinent details. The visit stressed preserving cultural heritage. It was a valuable experience for future projects.

From the selection of the venue to the coordination of the program, we made a detailed plan. We assigned different roles to each team member to ensure smooth operations.




During my time in Form 6, I actively participated in sports at the district level, where numerous schools came together to compete. Engaging in these sports events was an exciting and fulfilling experience. We had the opportunity to showcase our skills and talents while representing our school.
